Last updated: 2026-03-05

`CraftIDE`, the CraftIDE logo, and related brand elements are trademarks of ali975.

## Allowed without separate permission

- Truthful statements such as "compatible with CraftIDE" or "fork of CraftIDE".
- Non-commercial community discussion, tutorials, and reviews that do not imply official endorsement.

## Not allowed without written permission

- Using "CraftIDE" in a product name, installer name, domain, or branding in a way that implies official status.
- Reusing CraftIDE logos, app icons, or visual brand assets for redistributed builds.
- Marketing or selling a fork/repackaged build as the official CraftIDE product.

## Fork and redistribution naming rule

If you redistribute a modified or unmodified build, you must:

- Use a clearly different product name and icon.
- State prominently that your build is unofficial and not endorsed by ali975.
- Keep all AGPL and copyright notices intact.

To request trademark permission, open a GitHub issue in the main repository.
